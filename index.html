About Me

I am a game designer with hands-on experience in Unity and other game engines, level design, and gameplay systems. I work on building game spaces, mechanics, and player flow, turning ideas into clear and playable results. For me, design is not only about how a level looks, but how it makes the player feel. If a level does not create the right emotions, then the design is not complete. I focus on tension, atmosphere, and player reactions, making sure each moment has a purpose.

From my experience, the levels and gameplay moments I create consistently deliver the intended emotional impact, helping the overall game feel stronger and more engaging.
